About

Exit to the park and promenade

Nagatinskaya Floodplain is included in the territory of prospective development. The district is built with a balance of residential buildings, parks and recreational areas. The Moskva River protects the peninsula from the hustle and bustle of the center. You are connected to the city by good subway and road links, and in the future there will be water links to get anywhere in the city with ease.
